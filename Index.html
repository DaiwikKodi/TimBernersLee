I am the founder of www also known as the world wide web or the internet

Hello I am Tim Berners-Lee also known as "The Father of the internet" here are some fun facts about me

Tim Berners-Lee, a British scientist, invented the World Wide Web which is known as the WWW in 1989 while working at CERN.

CERN stands for the European Council for Nuclear Research.

The web was originally conceived and developed to meet the demand for automated information-sharing between scientists in universities and institutes around the world.

It is basically like a huge messaging app eg: Whatsapp.
The project for the WWW was supported and funded by the US Mission in Geneva through the CERN.
